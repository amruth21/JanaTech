How to use the program:

  1. When you first open the program, you must choose what forms you want to fill out. Select the "Add New Forms" button to do so.
  2. Select the "Choose Files" button. A pop-up window will appear where you can choose PDFs and Word Documents from your computer to be filled out. You can choose multiple files. Then, select "Upload Files" to finish.
  3. Click "Fill Forms" to continue, or select the back button to add more files.
  4. After selecting "Fill Forms," the program will find all common information in the forms you selected. Simply fill out your information once in the fields presented to you and that information will be filled in for all of the forms. If you've used the program before, your previous input will be remembered and can be changed here.
  5. The forms are now complete and ready for you. There are multiple ways for you to receive them. "Download" downloads the forms onto your computer for you to print. Or, you cna input your email and select "Email" for the forms to be emailed as attached files to you.

What to do with the filled out Form:

  1. Once the forms are done and delivered to you, print the forms.
  2. Deliver the forms to the department chair.
  3. Then, deliver the forms to Ms. Croner.
